People around you

looking more than just pretty things

Posted by the archiver on September 21, 2022 · 1 min read

It is easy to be caught up by work, life and everything in between. This seems to be all too relatable. The fact that I am in a college town and am a college student just enables me to simply turn a blind eye to the community around me. There is no point, as the lack of wisdom goes, of the people around you but your peers. Plus, it helps that they are young and pretty and humans just have the penchant of seeking out beauty.

Though it might sound innocuous, from an economic point of view, my indifference was extremely costly. The opportunities that I could have explored, the people that I would otherwise know if I hadn't lived in a world filled only by the immediate gratifications of college life. I have never quite understood Brent's ruminations about community; I swear it is a preoccupation that I have never shared with him but it was in this obscure coffee shop where I saw the pieces falling together. People that are secretly running this city, without the diabolical predisposition that we often associate them with.

Maybe I should pay attention to my community, whatever that is. Perhaps a moment knowing that I have been the puller of strings, one that does not control but brings us together. It was interesting, and I hope I remember this. With time weathering memory, I just wanted to note that whatever reason the reader might be here for, I hope he remembers that there is more than just this, there are people around you.
