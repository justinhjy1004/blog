Sweet Nothings

Posted by jhojy on December 01, 2022 · 1 min read

The rumbling sound of the rolling cart, the incessant noise from strangers perusing the aisles, and I was looking at you, reading the wandering gaze, knowing very well what, where, why... Just the intrigue of what can be, a revival of a ritual we have lost. An innocuous intermission of grabbing groceries. That is what I call a sweet nothing.
